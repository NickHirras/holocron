# Notification Providers

Holocron supports multiple notification channels to ensure teams stay engaged with their ceremonies.

## Corporate Chat (Slack/Teams)

**Use Case:** 
Check-in Reminders & Daily Updates. This is the primary "workspace" where engineers already live.

**Open Rate / Urgency:**
High. Instant engagement without leaving the work context.

---

## Email

**Use Case:**
Summaries & Documentation. Sending the final "Artifact" of a retrospective or a weekly team-health report.

**Open Rate / Urgency:**
Low. Good for record-keeping but too slow for "Standup is starting now!"
